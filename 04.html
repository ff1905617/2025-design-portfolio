MOVE! MFA Boston

Objective:

Create an experience for an exhibit about commuting around the world at MFA Boston. The experience should consist of four components: A desktop website, a mobile augmented reality experience, wayfinding and a social media campaign. Create a promotional video that showcases these four components.

Tools:

Illustrator, Figma, After Effects

Role:

Art Director/Experience Designer

Duration:

January 2025-March 2025

Mission Statement:

MOVE! is an exhibit at MFA Boston that showcases art about commuting from around the world. The average person spends at least an hour a day commuting. Although we all do it differently, we all have more in common than you might think.
