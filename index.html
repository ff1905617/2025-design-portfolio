I’m a designer based in Boston and a Senior in Communication Design at Massachusetts College of Art and Design. I’m a Massachusetts native and have lived here my whole life. Outside of design, I love gardening, music and drawing. My background is in manufacturing, however, I decided to make a career shift in 2023 and pursue design because it aligned more with the things I love.


I started at Massart in Fall of 2023 and fell in love with design, particularly for the web. I taught myself HTML, CSS and a little bit of Javascript while working on a personal project and became fascinated with building content for the web that inspires people to be more creative. I am excited to learn more and continue to expand my skillset. As a designer, I aim to create interesting, yet usable web-based experiences because who says functional design has to be boring?
